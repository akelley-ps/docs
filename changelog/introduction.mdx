## Versioning

We follow semantic versioning (SemVer) for our releases:

- **Major versions** (1.0.0, 2.0.0) - Breaking changes
- **Minor versions** (1.1.0, 1.2.0) - New features, backward compatible
- **Patch versions** (1.1.1, 1.1.2) - Bug fixes and minor improvements
